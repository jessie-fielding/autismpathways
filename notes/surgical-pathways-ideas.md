# Surgical Pathways App — Initial Ideas
Source: Untitleddocument(1).docx

1. **Aftercare Instructions Tool** — User takes a photo or uploads their aftercare instructions; AI parses and generates a structured care plan
2. **Pre-Op Instructions Tool** — Same flow for pre-operative instructions → AI-generated prep plan
3. **Symptom Checker Tool** — Interactive symptom checker
4. **Daily Healing Journey Log** — Daily observation log that compiles entries into formatted doctor notes for follow-up appointments
5. **AI Chat Tool** — Answers questions about symptoms and gauges whether the provider should be called (framed as suggestions, not medical advice — heavy disclaimers throughout)
